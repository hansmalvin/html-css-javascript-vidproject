Beranda Manfaat Latihan Menu Kembali

Chest:

Compound Movement: Incline bench press or variation (3 sets of 6-10 reps with medium load, rest 2-3 min).

Isolation Movement: Pec fly or variation (3 sets of 8-12 reps with light-medium load, rest 2-3 min).

Triceps:

Overhead Cable Extension: 3 sets of 6-10 reps with medium-heavy load, rest 2-3 min.

Pushdown: 3 sets of 6-10 reps with medium-heavy load, rest 2-3 min.

Back:

Pulldown Variation: 4 sets (1 warm-up, 3 working sets of 6-10 reps with medium-heavy load).

Row Variation: 3 sets of 8-12 reps with medium load.

Biceps:

Concentration Curl: 3 sets of 8-10 reps with medium load.

Lateral Raises: 4 sets of 12-15 reps with light load.

Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps with medium-heavy load.

Rear Delt Fly: 3 sets of 12-15 reps with light-medium load.

Squats: 4-5 sets of 8-10 reps with medium load.

Leg Curl: 3 sets of 10-12 reps with medium-heavy load.

Leg Extension: 3 sets of 12-15 reps with medium-heavy load.
